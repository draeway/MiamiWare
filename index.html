MiamiWare

About Us

MiamiWare is Miami’s #1 preferred Home Hardware store. Every year, Miami is rapidly expanding in population and broadband connections, and we aim to help you build the most comfortable home with the most secure systems. We have been serving Miami since 2001 and offer diverse products and services ranging from Home Hardware tools, to PC-building, and troubleshooting services. Our team of dedicated specialists will assist you with whatever needs and inquiries you may have. We offer delivery, pick-up, in-home services, and whatever caters to you.

Our Products and Services

Glue

We offer many styles of glue including white craft glue, super glue, spray glue, spray adhesives, and fabric adhesives. These are perfect for whatever crafting project you would like to create. Ask our staff to see which glue best suits your needs!

Tape

We offer many styles of tape including duct tape, double sided tape, masking tape, polyester tape, you name it. Tapes also come in different colours and quantity amounts. Ask our staff to see which tape best suits your needs!

Light Bulbs

We offer many different types of light bulbs, including Incandescent, Fluorescent, LED, and more. Bulbs come in packs of 6, 12, and 24. You may also take a look at our offering of lamps, as they come in all different shapes, sizes, and purposes!

Computer Repairs

Our team has an array of professionals that are here, at your service, to help you figure out and repair your electronics. We are highly trained in computing systems with years of education and references available on request. Satisfaction Guaranteed!
